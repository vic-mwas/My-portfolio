Projects

Masakani – Real Estate Platform

Masakani is a full-scale real estate platform consisting of a Web App, Mobile App, and Admin Dashboard. It enables agents to upload properties, admins to verify listings, and users to unlock premium content through secure payment workflows.

  • Flutter (Web & Android)
  • Firebase Firestore & Storage
  • Node.js & TypeScript (Cloud Functions)
  • Payment-gated content unlocking
  • Role-based access (User, Agent, Admin)
  • API testing & debugging using Postman

Web App: https://masakani.app
Android App: Google Play Closed Testing (Early Access)

Monevo – Expense Tracking App

Monevo is a production-ready expense tracking application designed for simplicity and performance. It supports offline-first usage with local data persistence and real-time UI updates.

  • Flutter & Dart
  • ISAR Local Database
  • Provider State Management
  • Production deployment on Google Play

Status: Live on Google Play (Production)
